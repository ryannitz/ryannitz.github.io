    Goals

    • Go to bed before 1am - This will allow me to get a plentiful night of sleep to reduce napping and furhter deterioration of my schedule.
    • Wake up at 9am or earlier - This will ensure I am ready for work in the morning and don't need to shift my schedule to accomodate morning activites
    • Reduce and elminate naps - This will prevent going to bed at later times from not being tired. It will also solidify a routine.

    Methods

    • Track actual sleeping stats through fitbit and software. This will be the primary feature.
    • Consume coffee and do other activities in place of naps
    • Go to bed at 1am latest. (Spoiler alert: This was not followed.)
    • Track a daily log to understand the conclusions in why I've succeeded or failed.
    • Understand urges and reasons for the behavior taking place.
