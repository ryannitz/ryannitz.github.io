Notes:

  • After selecting a number box, you can use arrow keys to increase/decrease values.
  • Mouse scroll while hovering over left and right side of a knob will increase/decrease the value.
  • You can spin the knob to change value. OR you can grab anywhere within the knob and drag the mouse up OR down to change values. Any combination of up AND down will cause funky behavior
